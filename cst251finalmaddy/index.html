This is a hearty vegetarian dish that any college student can make in the morning. The recipe calls for a teaspoon of olive oil, 4 eggs, 1 avocado, 4 slices of whole grain bread, cup of alfalfa sprouts, ¼ teaspoon of salt, ¼ teaspoon of pepper, and ½ cup salsa. However, any of these ingredients can be tailored to your personal desires. You then toast your bread and spread avocado over the toasts. Top with the sprouts. Then crack the eggs into an olive-oil coated pan and cook for a total of 4 minutes. Once the eggs are cooked to your desired liking place them on top of the toast. Finish off with the salt, pepper, and salsa. Enjoy! This recipe is quick and takes less than 10 minutes depending on your cooking skills. This recipe requires very few ingredients which is perfect for a college student on a budget. One Fried Egg and Avocado Toast is one serving that is only 247 calories with 11grams of protein and 6grams of fiber.
