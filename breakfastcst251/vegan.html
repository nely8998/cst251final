ACAI BOWL

Ingredients:

  • 1 sliced frozen peach
  • 1 medium unpeeled banana, frozen
  • 2 (3.5-ounce) packs frozen unsweetened açaí
  • 1/2 Fuji apple, unpeeled, in large dice
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • For topping (optional):
  • Fresh blueberries
  • Sliced kiwi
  • Flax seeds

Instructions

Put all the smoothie bowl ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth—you’ll end up with a thick, smoothie-like texture.

Blueberry and Banana Bread Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups plant-based milk, such as almond, soy, or coconut
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 medium size banana, peeled
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

The night before, combine the milk, oats, and chia seeds in a lidded container or large jar. For the blueberry and banana oats, mash the banana, and mix it into the oats with the cinnamon. Store in the fridge overnight. The next morning, mix the oats again to ensure everything is evenly distributed. Arrange the oats and the remaining ingredients in 2 bowls or jars. Enjoy

Green smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • Spinach
  • Almond Milk
  • kiwi
  • celery

Instructions

Add all the ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth. Best when fresh, enjoy immediately or keep in an airtight container in the fridge for 1 to 2 days.

Dragonfruit Smoothie
