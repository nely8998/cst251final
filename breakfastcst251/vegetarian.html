Vegetarian

There are many vegetarian breakfasts options out there, but not all of them are quick and easy to make. As a college student it is important to find healthy meals that are quick and easy. Below is four different vegetarian breakfast options to get you through your day.

Fried Egg & Avocado Toasts

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 4 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 cup of alfalfa sprouts
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Instructions

This recipe is quick and takes less than 10 minutes depending on your cooking skills. This recipe requires very few ingredients which is perfect for a college student on a budget. First toast your bread and spread avocado over the toasts. Top with the sprouts. Then crack the eggs into an olive-oil coated pan and cook for a total of 4 minutes. Once the eggs are cooked to your desired liking place them on top of the toast. Finish off with the salt, pepper, and salsa. Enjoy!

Easy Coffee Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cups of cooled coffee
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 1-2 tablespoons of vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon of agave syrup
  • 1/2 cup of ice

Instructions

This is a quick recipe that is for the multitasker that needs protein and coffee to start their day off right. Take all of the ingredients and blend them together. Enjoy!

Instructions
